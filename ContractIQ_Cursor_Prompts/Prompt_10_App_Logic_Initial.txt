In app.js:

- On load: show ContractTable
- On click of “View by…” → load ProviderTable with selected contract
- On click “Go to PCP Panel” → load MemberPanel
- On “View Quality Gaps” → show QualityModal
- Use `document.getElementById("section-id").classList.add("hidden")` to toggle views

Structure sections in HTML using IDs: #contractView, #providerView, #memberView
